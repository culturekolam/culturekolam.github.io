CSAE (Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation) Protection Policy

Anbu - Kolam Culture — Last updated: 2025

Anbu - Kolam Culture is a dating application designed exclusively for adults aged 18 and above. This document describes our policies, technical and operational controls to prevent, detect, and report Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). It is intended to be published externally as our formal standards and public-facing commitment.

1. Zero-Tolerance

We maintain a zero-tolerance stance toward any content, behaviour, or account that involves the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Any profile, image, video or message sexualizing a minor.
  • Attempts to arrange sexual activity with minors or facilitate sexual services involving minors.
  • Grooming behaviour targeted at anyone under 18 years.

2. Age Restriction & Verification

  • The service is strictly for users aged 18+; we reject registrations from users identified as under 18.
  • We deploy an age-gate at first launch and require date-of-birth during account creation.
  • Suspected minor accounts are suspended and removed pending investigation; evidence submitted by minors is not retained longer than necessary for investigation and compliance.

3. Content Moderation & Detection

We operate a layered moderation program that includes automated detection and human review:

  • Automated detection: Image and video scanning to flag explicit sexual content and potential underage subjects using industry-standard detection models.
  • Human review: Trained trust & safety reviewers examine flagged content and accounts, and apply enforcement actions.
  • Photo verification: Optional selfie or ID verification flows to reduce fake accounts and underage entry.
  • Proactive monitoring: Behavioural analytics to detect grooming patterns, rapid friend requests, message templates, or attempts to move conversations off-platform.

4. Reporting, Triage & Enforcement

  • Users may report accounts, messages, or content via in-app "Report" controls on profiles and chat screens.
  • All CSAE reports are prioritised for immediate triage by our trust & safety team.
  • Confirmed violations result in immediate account suspension, content removal, and permanent ban.
  • Where required by law, confirmed CSAE cases are reported to law enforcement or relevant agencies promptly and with lawful cooperation.

5. Evidence Preservation & Data Sharing

  • We securely retain evidence necessary for investigations and law enforcement requests for a limited period, using encrypted storage with strict access controls.
  • Access to CSAE-related data is limited to authorised personnel only.
  • We comply with legal requests for data disclosure arising from law enforcement investigations, subject to applicable law.

6. Privacy & Data Safety

We describe our data collection and handling practices in the app Privacy Policy and the Google Play Data safety form. Measures include minimal data collection, secure storage, and user controls for account deletion and data export.

7. User Education & Safety Resources

  • The app includes in-app safety tips, a safety centre with guidance on recognising grooming and abuse, and step-by-step instructions on reporting.
  • We provide links to regional child-protection hotlines and resources where available.

8. Continuous Improvement

We regularly update detection models, review enforcement KPIs, and invest in training for moderators to align with global best practices and regulatory requirements.
